Create Tournament

Tournaments are competitive events where players fight to prove their skills against other players! Anyone may create or enter a tournament, but know that others will be playing to win! :)
  • Constructed: Players provide their own constructed decks with no limits beyond the selected format.
  • Sealed: Players will receive standardized starter decks with digital boosters and 30 minutes (by default) to crack those packs and build a deck for the tournament.
  • Live Draft: Players will sit around the table and open digital packs, selecting 1 card and passing the rest to their opponents. After all packs have been drafted, players have 15 minutes (by default) to construct a deck for the tournament.
  • Solo Draft: Players will each draft against the computer, meaning that they have a limited selection but the remainder is not passed to a human opponent. After drafting, players have 30 minutes (by default) to construct a deck for the tournament.
